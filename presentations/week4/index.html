Competitors

Brands

Nestle and Hershey.

Strengths

Nestle: Variety of flavours and bars, worldwide distribution, strong brand equity.
Hershey: Classic American values and branding, and of course, their theme park, Hersheypark!

Weaknesses

Both of your competitors have sacrificed quality for quantity, and that will be their downfall. While they are both very popular, they are not known for having the best tasting chocolate.

Logo Inspiration

How the logo shows what the company is about

The inspiration for my logo will be the tail feathers of the parrot. The tail is the most important part of the bird, as it allows the bird balance, control, and a cool butt. Therefore, I will focus on the tail for my logo.

Why the logo is effective for the brand

This logo will be effective because it will bring bright colours, brilliant imagery, and classic design all together in one cohesive logo.

How the branding/logo makes us stand out

You will stand out because my design is both classic and contemporary, making it perfect to create shock and awe, while still feeling comfortable and approachable.
